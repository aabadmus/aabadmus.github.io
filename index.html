About Sortube

Sortube is a project born out of our passion for enhancing the YouTube experience. As avid YouTube users, we often felt overwhelmed by the vast amount of content available. Therefore, we set out to create a platform that simplifies content discovery, personalization, and playlist management on YouTube.

This project is a Portfolio Project for Holberton School, where we have been honing our software development skills. It showcases our ability to create innovative solutions and build user-friendly applications.
